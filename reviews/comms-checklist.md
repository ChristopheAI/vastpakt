# Comms Checklist

Use this checklist for email, LinkedIn, client replies, public comments, and outreach messages.

- The message answers the actual sender, post, client, or audience context.
- No public action is implied as already taken unless it was actually taken.
- The tone fits the channel and relationship.
- The opening line is specific to the real situation, not generic rapport-building.
- Claims, promises, deadlines, prices, and commitments are sourced or explicitly tentative.
- The message separates what is known from what is being proposed.
- The next step is clear and does not create avoidable friction.
- Sensitive or high-stakes details are not guessed from names, memory, or partial context.
