# Lead log

Use this only for non-sensitive lead metadata. Do not paste full submitted messages, private emails, phone numbers, attachments, or client-identifying details into this file.

## Fields

- `date_received`: date the lead arrived.
- `source`: where the lead came from, such as contact form, email, LinkedIn, referral, or other.
- `classification`: usable situation, too vague, wrong fit, spam/test.
- `situation_object`: mail, offerte, Excel, CRM, factuur, afspraak, dossier, other.
- `reply_within_24h`: yes, no, or pending.
- `follow_up_conversation`: yes, no, or pending.
- `copy_signal`: short non-identifying phrase or pattern worth reviewing later.
- `notes`: operational note without private message content.
