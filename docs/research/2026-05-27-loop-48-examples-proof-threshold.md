# Loop 48 - Examples Proof Threshold

## Question

Where is the homepage still stronger than its proof after another Exa and Swipefile weak-spot pass?

## Exa source signals

- Archie / Atlantic Belgium: information moved through Word, Excel, e-mail and phone, with multiple handover moments between quote request, sales, after sales and accounting. Errors included a forgotten discount, wrong invoice, missed quotation follow-up, and time spent figuring out where things went wrong.
  - URL: https://en.archie.nl/klantcase-atlantic-belgium
- Eezee-it / De Ster: operations were manual and relied on emails, Excel spreadsheets and paper-based processes, creating limited visibility into stock, order management and invoicing.
  - URL: https://eezee-it.com/customer-cases/de-ster-141
- We Are / Symons: price calculations were manual, data was not connected, document export was inefficient, and every change had to be made in multiple places.
  - URL: https://we-are.be/cases/symons
- Codelines / bouwsector ERP: losse Excel-bestanden, copy-paste, herinvoer van standaardgegevens, manuele berekeningen, geen versiebeheer, geen traceerbaarheid, en geen centraal overzicht voor leads, projecten en facturen.
  - URL: https://www.codelines.be/cases/maatwerk-erp-bouwsector/

## Swipefile discipline used

- Keep proof close to claims.
- Let a concrete before/after or work object carry the claim.
- Do not let a recognizable scenario read like measured proof when only one named case is proven.

## Verdict

The weak spot is the examples section heading.

`Vier plekken waar tijd verloren gaat tussen werk, tools en documenten.` is recognizable and uses market-supported `tijd verloren gaat`, but it can read like four proven/measured loss points. On the current page, only BAVAST is a named worked case; the first three cards are useful recognition scenarios.

## Change

Change the section heading to:

`Vier situaties waar je opnieuw moet zoeken, overnemen of rondvragen.`

## Classification

- `Vier situaties`: light edit / proof-threshold correction.
- `zoeken, overnemen of rondvragen`: existing homepage workday language, reinforced by Exa patterns around information moving through Word, Excel, mail, phone, manual copy-paste, handovers and disconnected data.

## Remaining proof gap

The first three example cards remain plausible source-led scenarios, not public case proof. Stronger named proof needs user-provided cases or approved anonymized examples.
