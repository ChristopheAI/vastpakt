# Loop 54 - First Response Alignment

## Question

After loop 53, does the surrounding homepage copy still weaken or duplicate the new first-response artifact?

## Finding

Yes. Two older lines still framed the first step as only a fit check:

- `Dan krijg je eerlijk terug of dit past.`
- `Dan zie je of dit past.`

That was weaker than the new public promise: a short first look on the submitted example.

## Change

- In the Eerste Zicht exchange card, changed `Wij sturen terug` to `Kort eerste zicht`.
- In the Past dit note, changed the return promise to `Dan krijg je een kort eerste zicht op jouw voorbeeld.`
- In the contact intro, changed `Dan zie je of dit past.` to `Eén echte plek met tijdverlies is genoeg om te starten.`

## Classification

- `Kort eerste zicht`: first-response artifact, now aligned across offer/person/contact.
- `op jouw voorbeeld`: boundary phrase, keeps the promise limited to one submitted example.
- `genoeg om te starten`: threshold language, not a new deliverable claim.

## Boundary

This loop does not add report, audit, roadmap, score, ROI, savings, implementation, or automation language.
