# Loop 79 - Eerste Zicht Deliverables Compression

## Question

After loop 78 made the offer block more action-oriented, what still makes `Eerste Zicht` feel heavier than one small first step?

## Sources checked

- `vastpakt-direct-response-copy-grill`
- Current `Eerste Zicht` offer block
- Copy classification through loop 78
- Experiments through loop 78

## Verdict

The direct CTA inside `Eerste Zicht` improved the route, but the adjacent `Wat wordt duidelijk` list still had five bullets.

The list was truthful, but it could make the small first step feel like a mini-analysis package:

- several work objects;
- status, proof and next step;
- transferability;
- where to begin.

That is useful internally, but too much at the moment where the visitor should feel that one example is enough.

## Change

Compressed the list to three action-critical points:

1. where the example hangs;
2. which question becomes visible first;
3. whether this fits as a next step.

## Why this is safe

- No new claim.
- No new proof.
- No new response promise.
- The contact block still explains what to send and what comes back.
- The page still supports hesitant readers, but the offer block feels lighter.

## Boundary

Do not turn this block into an audit, roadmap, report, checklist or intake package. Keep it as a short explanation of what becomes clear from one example.
