# Loop 39 - Person Trust Pattern Review

## Question

How should Vastpakt decide whether and where to show the person behind the company, using Exa market patterns and Swipefile proof-placement discipline, without writing public homepage copy yet?

## Verdict

The person block should not become a biography section.

For Vastpakt, the stronger job is contact-friction reduction: who reads the example, who replies, whether the visitor is dealing directly with the person doing the work, and what kind of first response they can expect.

That means the person block belongs near the action point, not in the hero and not as a broad about-me section above the work examples.

## Swipefile Discipline

- Proof should sit close to the doubt it answers. The person behind Vastpakt mainly answers doubt at the contact moment.
- A person block should not brag. It should show what changes for the visitor: direct handling, clear first response, no handoff, and a bounded next step.
- One concrete operating fact is stronger than a full biography.
- The first-contact exchange should stay small. The person block should make sending one example feel safer, not make the page heavier.
- Every section must reduce doubt. The person block's doubt category is `Action` and `Safety`, not `Recognition`.

## Placement Decision

Recommended placement: inside the contact section, or directly between FAQ and contact.

Reason: the reader already understands the workday problem, examples, offer shape, fit boundaries, and FAQ. The remaining question is whether sending a real example is safe and useful.

Not recommended in the hero: the hero's job is the workday scene and first recognition.

Not recommended above the examples: it interrupts proof and recognition before the visitor has enough reason to care about the person.

Not recommended as a generic about section: it adds page weight without resolving the contact decision.

## Facts Needed Before Public Copy

- Public name format.
- Public role/title.
- Whether a real photo is available and wanted.
- Who reads submitted examples.
- Who replies.
- Expected reply window, if any.
- What `direct` means in this service context.
- Whether Vastpakt wants to promise an honest no, redirect, or fit boundary.
- One factual experience/proof anchor that is safe to publish.
- First person or third person voice.

## Decision

No homepage edit yet.

Loop 39 should only document the decision: person-as-trust belongs near the contact action, and public copy should wait until the factual handling details are known.
