## Most Important Finding

The main weakness is not tone. It is offer certainty.

Vastpakt is right to avoid sounding like software op maat, AI automation, ERP, or process consulting. But if Vastpakt chooses a smaller first step, that first step must become more concrete than the competitors' larger offers.

## Competitor Pattern

- Axxit gives a simple buyer shape: from Excel and loose tools to one working system.
- Flowlift gives a commercial shape: short call, time wasters, plan, price ranges, quick win, KPIs.
- Codelines gives a consulting/productized shape: scan, analysis, report, action plan.
- ERP op maat gives a heavier implementation shape: ERP/CRM/WMS/EDI from quote to delivery and invoice.
- DatApps gives breadth: many software categories, but less sharpness.
- Sitesoft gives sober strategic filtering: when maatwerk is and is not right.

## Strict Verdict

`Stuur één voorbeeld` is strong as a low-friction entry, but weak if it remains only a filter.

The visitor can still wonder:

- what does Christophe actually send back?
- is the 24-hour response a fit check, a mini diagnosis, a call proposal, or advice?
- is it free, paid, or the start of paid Eerste Zicht work?
- where does first contact stop and Vastpakt work begin?

## Autoresearch Guardrail

Before the next public copy change around contact, person block, or Eerste Zicht, answer this operating question:

```text
What exactly does someone receive within 24 hours after sending one example?
```

Do not fill this gap with invented anti-sales copy or vague reassurance. The next useful homepage loop should either:

1. get the missing operating facts from Christophe; or
2. weaken the 24-hour promise until it matches what is actually true.

## Candidate Shape If True

Only if operationally true, the first response can be framed as:

- whether this fits;
- where the work seems to come loose;
- which one question should become visible first;
- what the smallest next step is.

This is not approved public copy yet. It is a shape to verify.

## Remaining Uncertainty

The page cannot safely state a stronger first-response promise until the exact format, cost boundary, and next-step boundary are confirmed.
