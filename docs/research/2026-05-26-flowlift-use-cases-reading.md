## What Flowlift Does Well

Flowlift makes use cases easy to understand because each case has a familiar shape:

- specific business type;
- one concrete operational pain;
- a named before-state;
- clear implementation steps;
- visible time window;
- measurable outcome;
- direct CTA for the same use case.

The homepage also groups use cases by practical work areas:

- support and email;
- sales and quotations;
- invoices and reporting;
- customer service;
- inventory;
- Excel to CRM sync;
- Outlook and Teams follow-up;
- CRM to Exact.

That grouping is useful because the reader does not need to understand the full service category first. They can enter through a recognizable work situation.

## What Vastpakt Should Not Copy

Flowlift is explicitly selling automation, AI, integration, KPI, ROI, and time saving. That is not Vastpakt's first category.

Do not copy:

- automation-first framing;
- large quantified claims before proof exists;
- "AI transformed the process" language;
- fake ROI theatre;
- broad technology lists as trust proof;
- "win hours back" as the main promise.

Vastpakt must stay one step earlier:

```text
Before automation: what is actually loose?
```

## Transfer To Vastpakt

The useful transfer is the use-case structure, not the positioning.

For each Vastpakt proof card, use this adapted case grammar:

1. `Werkdag`: where this shows up in normal work.
2. `Voor`: what is currently loose.
3. `Ontbreekt`: owner, status, proof, reminder, approval, route, fallback, or stopmoment.
4. `Situatiekaart`: what gets put on the table.
5. `Na`: what someone can now point to, hand over, or decide.
6. `Vervolg`: which one small piece can be fixed after zicht.

This keeps Vastpakt concrete without pretending the first step is already automation.

## Strong Vastpakt Use-Case Families

Flowlift's categories confirm that Vastpakt's first proof families are right, but the names should stay operational:

- `Opvolging`: email, meeting, quote, customer response, reminder.
- `Bewijs naar geld`: quote, approval, delivery, timesheet, invoice trigger.
- `Overdraagbaarheid`: Excel/CRM sync, Teams/Outlook follow-up, person absent, work handoff.
- `Werk naast systemen`: data exists in tools, but meaning and next step do not travel.

## Better Hero Implication

Flowlift starts with the desired result. Vastpakt should start with the recognizable loose moment.

Better for Vastpakt:

```text
We blijven opvolgen, maar niemand ziet waar het staat.
```

Then the proof object:

```text
Status, eigenaar, bewijs, volgende stap en wat er gebeurt als iemand wegvalt.
```

Then the boundary:

```text
Eerst tonen we waar het loshangt. Daarna kiezen we samen één stuk dat we vastpakken.
```

## Guardrail

Use cases must not turn Vastpakt into Flowlift-lite.

Vastpakt should not say:

```text
Wij automatiseren uw routine.
```

It should say:

```text
Wij maken zichtbaar welke routine vandaag op geheugen, losse mails of onduidelijke afspraken draait.
```
