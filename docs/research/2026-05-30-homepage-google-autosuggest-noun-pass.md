# Homepage Google Autosuggest Noun Pass

Date: 2026-05-30

## Loop Question

Carry the Over Vastpakt market-language pass into the homepage without doing a broad rewrite.

## Verdict

Better approach available: do not rewrite the whole homepage. Replace only the high-visibility nouns that still read like internal Vastpakt language.

## Google Autosuggest Signals

- `offerte opvolging`
- `offerte opvolging mail`
- `offerte opvolging mail voorbeeld`
- `opvolging offerte voorbeeld`
- `opvolgingsmail offerte`
- `facturen bijhouden`
- `facturen bijhouden excel`
- `klantenbestand excel`
- `klantenbestand excel template`
- `klantenbestand excel maken`
- `excel klantenbestand voorbeeld`

## Exa Market Signals

- KMO-Facturatie uses ordinary KMO words around `facturatie`, `offertes`, `betalingsopvolging`, `klantenbestand`, and getting an answer within 24 hours.
- Afandi frames the route as `facturen`, `offertes`, `openstaande saldi`, `openstaande facturen`, and avoiding `manuele overschrijving`.
- UNIVO uses `CRM`, `facturatie`, `offertes`, `klantendatabase`, `lead management en opvolging`, and `openstaande facturen opvolgen`.
- DeskManager names `facturen`, `planning`, `klantgegevens`, `offertes`, `klantenbeheer`, `opvolging`, and info spread across Excel, agendas and notes.

## Classification

- `offerte opvolging`: market language / Google Autosuggest.
- `facturen bijhouden excel`: market language / Google Autosuggest.
- `klantenbestand excel`: market language / Google Autosuggest.
- `klantenbestand in Excel`: light edit for public readability.
- `openstaande factuur`: market language from adjacent KMO software pages.
- `offerte die je moet opvolgen`: light edit of Google Autosuggest terms `offerte opvolging` and `offerte opvolgen`.

## Homepage Change

Changed the recurring homepage examples where they carry recognition:

- `Excel-rij` -> `klantenbestand in Excel`
- `factuurroute` -> `openstaande factuur`
- plain `offerte` in the hero/offer example -> `offerte die je moet opvolgen`
- `status, eigenaar, bewijs of volgende stap` in top metadata/hero lede -> `opvolging, akkoord, status of laatste actie`

## Not Changed

- Lower FAQ/model language remains for a later pass.
- No all-in-one software promise was added.
- No generic automation, workflow, or process-optimization claim was added.
