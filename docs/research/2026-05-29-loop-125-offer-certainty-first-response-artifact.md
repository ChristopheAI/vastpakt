
## Evidence Lenses (Exa)

Steal the mechanism, not the style.

### A - Name the exact artifact, timeframe, and contents

Source: Wolrix free audit (`https://www.wolrix.com/free-audit`).

> "Free AI Architecture Audit, 3-Page PDF in 24 Hours... The PDF includes: an honest read, the three highest-leverage problems, and a recommended next step... The PDF is the deliverable. No sales call required. Or we tell you we're not the right fit."

Useful signal: certainty comes from naming the concrete thing that lands in the inbox, its timeframe, its contents, and an honest no. High decision utility.

### A - A named result beats a vague consultation

Source: The Proposition Architect (`https://thepropositionarchitect.com/`).

> "A scored diagnostic. A named failure pattern. A ranked fix sequence."

Useful signal: the offer reads as a specific deliverable, not "we will look at it".

### B - Specificity builds more trust than hype; do not give away the full fix

Sources: ALM clinic model (`https://almcorp.com/blog/clinic-model-for-audits-digital-agencies/`); Alli AI free-audit guide (`https://www.alliai.com/seo-agency-academy/how-to-do-seo-audits`); LeadGenCrypto teardown (`https://leadgencrypto.com/blog/crypto-outreach/ai-teardown-before-first-email-crypto-projects/`).

> "Specificity builds more trust than hype." / "A free sample of your expertise, not the entire meal." / "Three observations and one implication are enough."

Useful signal: the first response is a complete small piece of work that stands alone, but it is not the paid deliverable. Keep it a first look, not the whole fix.

## Mechanism

- **What works:** the first response is a named, concrete artifact with stated contents, a timeframe, and an honest no.
- **What Vastpakt can borrow:** name the thing ("een korte geschreven eerste blik"), keep the 24-hour timeframe already claimed elsewhere, name the three contents, keep the honest no.
- **What Vastpakt must not copy:** audit/scan/diagnostic jargon, a PDF-with-page-count gimmick, benchmarks or metrics ("200+ websites", ROI), "highest-leverage", or a transformation-framework pitch. The DESIGN.md anti-references forbid all of these.
- **Why it works:** it qualifies fit before either side commits time and gives the visitor a useful artifact regardless. Certainty comes from knowing exactly what arrives.

## Change

Rewrote the "Wij sturen terug" copy to name the artifact:

```text
Een korte geschreven eerste blik op dat ene voorbeeld, binnen 24 uur. Waar het blijft hangen,
welke vraag eerst zichtbaar moet worden, en de kleine volgende stap. Past het niet, dan zeggen we dat.
```

One bounded change. No other section touched.
