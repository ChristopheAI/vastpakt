# Loop 80 - Eerste Zicht Footer Line Removal

## Question

After loop 79 compressed `Wat wordt duidelijk`, does the footer sentence below the bullets still earn its place?

## Sources checked

- `vastpakt-direct-response-copy-grill`
- Current `Eerste Zicht` block after loop 79
- Copy classification through loop 79

## Verdict

The sentence `Eerst één voorbeeld helder krijgen voordat er iets groter van wordt.` was safe, but after loop 79 it became repetition.

The block already says:

- one example;
- what becomes clear;
- direct CTA to contact.

The footer sentence did not add proof, a clearer action, or a necessary objection resolution.

## Change

Removed the footer sentence under the `Wat wordt duidelijk` bullets.

## Why this is safe

- No new claim.
- No new proof.
- No new public term.
- The CTA remains visible.
- The three bullets still explain the first-step return value.

## Boundary

Do not replace the removed sentence with anti-sales or preparation language. The point is to reduce explanatory weight, not add another reassurance line.
