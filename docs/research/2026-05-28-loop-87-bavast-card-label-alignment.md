# Loop 87 - BAVAST Card Label Alignment

## Question

Why does the fourth examples card say `Uitgewerkt voorbeeld` instead of `Praktijkcase` like the other cards?

## Verdict

Historically it made sense. Now it is less coherent.

## Why It Was There

In loop 27, BAVAST appeared twice:

- compact proof under the check;
- a full examples-grid card.

The label `Uitgewerkt voorbeeld` prevented the page from repeating `Praktijkcase: BAVAST` too heavily when BAVAST was still carrying much of the proof weight.

## Why It Changed Now

The examples grid now has four real practice-case-style cards:

- werkplaatsvoorraad;
- merkwebsite;
- mailboxen;
- Word-rapport / BAVAST.

Because the section heading says `Praktijkvoorbeelden`, the fourth card should follow the same label pattern.

## Change

Changed:

`Uitgewerkt voorbeeld`

to:

`Praktijkcase: Word-rapport`

## Boundary

This does not add a new proof claim.

It also avoids repeating `Praktijkcase: BAVAST` as a label, because BAVAST is already named in the compact proof and in the card body.
