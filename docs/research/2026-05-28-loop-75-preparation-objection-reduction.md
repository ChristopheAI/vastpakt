# Loop 75 - Preparation Objection Reduction

## Question

Where can the visitor still think this becomes a big project, another tool, or a preparation-heavy intake?

## Sources checked

- `2026-05-28-psychological-triggers-workbook-reading.md`
- Current FAQ, qualification, person and contact blocks.
- Copy classification through loop 74.

## Trigger signal

The useful trigger is objection raising/resolution. The page should resolve real likely objections without inventing defensive copy.

Likely objection:

`What if I do not know where the problem is yet?`

This is close to preparation-heavy intake fear: the visitor may think they need to diagnose the problem before contacting Vastpakt.

## Verdict

The FAQ already had the right question:

`Wat als we nog niet weten waar het probleem zit?`

But the answer started with:

`Dan starten we kleiner.`

That is true, but it still frames the next step from Vastpakt's side. It is stronger to say what the visitor does not have to do first.

## Change

Changed:

`Dan starten we kleiner. Eén normale situatie is genoeg om te zien waar status, eigenaar, bewijs of volgende stap loskomt.`

to:

`Dan hoef je dat niet eerst uit te zoeken. Eén normale situatie is genoeg om te zien waar status, eigenaar, bewijs of volgende stap loskomt.`

## Why this is safe

- It resolves a real objection.
- It lowers the first-action threshold.
- It avoids banned/rejected preparation copy.
- It does not add a new promise, proof claim, report, audit, roadmap, score, implementation, or automation result.

## Boundary

Do not use invented anti-sales lines like `geen voorbereiding`, `geen projectbrief`, or similar. Keep the objection answer in ordinary workday language.
