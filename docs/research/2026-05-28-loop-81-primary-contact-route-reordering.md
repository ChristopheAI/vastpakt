# Loop 81 - Primary Contact Route Reordering

## Question

After `Eerste Zicht` was tightened, where does the route still slow down for a visitor who is ready to act?

## Sources checked

- `vastpakt-direct-response-copy-grill`
- Current homepage order after loop 80
- Copy classification through loop 80

## Verdict

The page had a working CTA from `Eerste Zicht` to contact, but the natural scroll route still placed five support sections before Christophe and the form.

That made the lower page feel like:

proof -> offer -> more explanation -> more explanation -> more explanation -> fit -> FAQ -> person -> contact

For direct response, the primary route should be:

proof -> offer -> person -> contact

Then support can follow for readers who keep scrolling.

## Change

Moved:

- person block;
- contact block.

New order:

`Eerste Zicht -> person -> contact -> why visible -> method -> connection -> fit -> FAQ`.

## Why this is safe

- No public copy promise changed.
- No new proof claim.
- No new offer layer.
- Contact path becomes easier after the offer.
- Support sections remain available below for hesitant readers.

## Boundary

Do not turn the support sections into a second pitch after contact. They should stay as optional proof/objection support, not a new route.
