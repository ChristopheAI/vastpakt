## Verdict

The next visual bottleneck is the Christophe/person block after contact. At the in-app browser width, the person block collapsed to one column and measured about 1272px tall.

Land-book's useful pattern is not a biography style. It is trust-module discipline: image and text remain connected as long as the viewport can support it.

## Borrow

- Keep person/trust content as one composed module on tablet/desktop widths.
- Let the photo and trust copy explain each other instead of stacking too early.
- Keep the lower page from feeling like a second long route after contact.

## Do Not Copy

- About-us spectacle.
- Personal brand storytelling.
- Large stats or social proof.
- Extra biography copy without facts.

## Public Change

No public copy changed.

The person block now keeps its two-column layout until the narrower `780px` breakpoint. It no longer collapses at `900px`.
