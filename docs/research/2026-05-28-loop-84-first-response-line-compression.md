# Loop 84 - First-Response Line Compression

## Question

What is the next loop after loop 83 if we do not choose by preference, but by Exa, competitor patterns, direct-response source notes and the current Vastpakt page?

## Verdict

Keep the first-response promise near the form, but compress it.

The line should not become vague. It should also not repeat the same work verbs already used on the `Stuur dit` side of the contact exchange.

## Competitor Pattern

Competitors tend to reduce contact uncertainty by naming one or more of:

- discovery call;
- first response timing;
- quote or estimate;
- audit;
- roadmap;
- action plan;
- budget range;
- project consultation;
- direct founder/developer contact.

That makes the first step clearer, but often heavier than Vastpakt should be.

## Direct-Response Source Check

Useful prior notes:

- Copywriter's Padawan: the next step should feel low-risk and concrete.
- No B.S. Direct Marketing: the offer needs a clear next action and follow-up expectation.
- Great Leads: offer-style clarity belongs near CTA/contact, not in the hero.
- Cashvertising: concrete examples and specificity work only when they reduce uncertainty rather than add effort.
- Psychological Triggers: strongest safe triggers are simplicity, specificity, integrity and objection handling.
- Web Copy That Sells: headings and first lines must carry the route; long object lists can become effort.

## Current Page Finding

The contact exchange had two jobs:

- `Stuur dit`: names the objects and friction: mail, offerte, Excel-rij, CRM-notitie, Word-document, afspraak, zoeken, overnemen, uitleg vragen.
- `Krijg dit terug`: repeated part of that friction with `waar je vandaag zoekt, overneemt of rondvraagt`.

The first-response promise still needs to include:

- within 24 hours;
- whether this fits;
- where the submitted example hangs;
- which question first needs to become visible.

## Change

Changed:

`Binnen 24 uur: of dit past, waar je vandaag zoekt, overneemt of rondvraagt, en welke vraag eerst zichtbaar moet worden.`

to:

`Binnen 24 uur: of dit past, waar je voorbeeld blijft hangen, en welke vraag eerst zichtbaar moet worden.`

## Boundary

This does not promise:

- audit;
- report;
- roadmap;
- quote;
- project estimate;
- action plan;
- automation advice;
- savings estimate;
- company-wide diagnosis.

It only compresses the first-response expectation around one submitted example.
