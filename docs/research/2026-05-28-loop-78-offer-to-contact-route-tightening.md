# Loop 78 - Offer-To-Contact Route Tightening

## Question

After applying the direct response copy grill, where does the route from proof to action still slow down?

## Sources checked

- `vastpakt-direct-response-copy-grill`
- `2026-05-28-web-copy-that-sells-reading.md`
- `2026-05-28-psychological-triggers-workbook-reading.md`
- Current homepage offer block
- Copy classification through loop 77

## Direct response signal

After the visitor has seen proof and recognized one possible example, the page should not make the next section feel like another thing to understand before acting.

The offer block should make the next action feel smaller:

- one example;
- what becomes clear;
- send it.

## Verdict

The `Eerste Zicht` section had the right public promise, but the `Daarna bekijken we` list added another explanatory step before the next action.

That list was true, but at this point in the route it could feel like intake preparation.

## Change

- Removed the `Daarna bekijken we` list.
- Added a direct `Stuur één voorbeeld` button inside the offer section.

## Why this is safe

- No new claim.
- No new proof.
- No new response promise.
- No new offer layer.
- The remaining deliverables still explain what becomes clear.
- The public route becomes lighter after the proof section.

## Boundary

Do not replace the removed list with anti-sales slogans or preparation language. The point is not to say what the visitor does not need. The point is to make the actual action easy.
